Placement Guidelines

What it takes to get hired
6 rules
01

Resume First

Your resume gets 6 seconds. One page, clear metrics, no fluff. We review every student's resume before they apply.

02

GitHub Portfolio

Companies check your code. Maintain clean repos with READMEs, commit history, and live demos where possible.

03

250+ Problems

That's the threshold where patterns start clicking. Easy → Medium → Hard. Consistency beats intensity.

04

Mock Interviews

Practice verbalizing your thought process. We pair you with mentors for weekly mock sessions.

05

System Design Basics

Even junior roles expect high-level design thinking. Learn scalability, caching, and database choices.

06

Apply Strategically

Don't spray and pray. Target 10 companies deeply rather than 100 shallowly. Research culture and stack.
